Jaden's Tree Fort

Welcome to Jaden's Tree Fort, this is a children's learning and fun activity website.

Navigate through the different pages using the colored tabs on the left hand side.

The site is divided up into two main sections for now: Reading, and Math. Each one of those sections will have different activities for your child, they will help them work on basic skills.

For now this site will work on kindergarden level skills, But check back all the time because we will be adding different activities later, and the skill level will increase

Click on any of the words in the reading pages to have the word read aloud to your child
