Jaden's Tree Fort

Welcome to Jaden’s Tree Fort, an interactive children’s learning site filled with fun and educational activities!

Currently all activities are based on Kindergarten skills, ranging from basic to advanced. Be sure to check back regularly as we add more and more activities, skill challenges and grade levels.

The site has two main sections for now: Reading and Mathematics. Use the colored tabs on the left side of the page to navigate through the different activities.

It is recommended that younger children have their help their child with the site until they are confident readers on their own
