Sight Words




Sight words are words that appear frequently in most of the text we read, they cannot be easily sounded out.

Learning these words helps children become more confident readers.

  1. One sight words will appear on the screan at a time, if your child reads it correctly click yes, if not click no.
  2. At the end a score will be issued along with a list of sight words that your child missed and needs to work on
